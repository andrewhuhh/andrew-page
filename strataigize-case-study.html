January 2024

Dressing up the hottest new marketing agency on the block.

Strataigize is a results-driven app marketing agency specializing in App Store Optimization (ASO) for mobile apps. Faced with challenges in brand perception, Strataigize aimed to refresh its visual identity to emerge as a reputable leader in the app marketing space.

I was brought on as the Creative Lead to spearhead a comprehensive rebranding and web redesign, with the goal of boosting organic traffic, enhancing sales, and solidifying their market reputation.

The Challenge

Un-roughing the diamond

Strataigize's existing branding lacked coherency and polish, which diminished its perceived value in a competitive market. The key challenges were

  • Inconsistency - The existing brand assets did not convey a unified message.
  • Outdated Visuals - The design failed to communicate the agency's innovative approach.
  • Perceived Value - The visuals did not align with the promise of expert app marketing and ASO excellence.

Brand Design

Visualizing the foundations of the brand

The first visual design challenge was to create a new logo. Over the course of ~2 weeks, I iterated through a number of different logographic concepts. Through trials, vibes and error, we finally landed on our winner. A sleek abstracted S shape that was both visually simple yet distinct.

Building the system

I extended the core visual themes we established in the logo design into tokens that set the foundation for the rest of our branding system.

  1. I settled on a black/white base shade with a striking orange accent colour.
  2. The Gilmer font was selected to compliment the logo's custom typography.
  3. A graphics schema was devised, composed of 3D illustrations that accompany important written content, and lifestyle photography that gives a more human touch to the brand ambience.

Packaging and Delivery

A freshly refined visual and web experience.

The refreshed visual identity was seamlessly integrated into the new website design built on Webflow, creating a coherent digital presence that resonates with Strataigize's target audience.
